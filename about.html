MISSION

Our mission is to develop young children with active and creative minds and the courage to act on their beliefs. We stress the total development of each child: spiritual, moral, intellectual, social, emotional, and physical. To empower club members to serve the communities, meet humanitarian needs, encourage peace and ``Be A Loving Lion``

VISION

To enable every individual regardless of ability to achieve their full potential, to prepare for future life and to become life long learners, developing a thirst for learning and to become good citizens equipped for the challenges of the 21st century

ACHIEVEMENTS

Pupils demonstrate good progress in terms of lateral progression and functional development, using the engagement model to greatly support. We celebrate small steps of achievement and recognise that all children learn differently and it is our responsibility to identify their learning style and needs.

 Boarding facilities

Our institution provides perfect spacious hygienic and secure rooms which gives boarders a good learning environment. Boarders are provided with warm bathing water as they enjoy the mentorship of dormitory supervisor.However food from outside is not allowed

 Education System

Our School is private Co-educational Day and boarding providing ECD,Lower and Upper Primary education.The school follows the Competence Based Curriculum and offers stimulating and caring environment, enabling your child to realize his/her full potential academically. confidence for a fulfilling and responsible life in a changing society. English and Kiswahili is the language of communication throught the school But many of our children have English as their second languange. We have quality boarding facilities for boys and girls from Grade 4 upwards. Transport is also provided for day-scholars but at a certain fee.
