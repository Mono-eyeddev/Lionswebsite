Our football team

Sports are not just about competition; they teach us valuable life skills like teamwork, discipline, perseverance, and sportsmanship. Whether you're a seasoned athlete or just starting out, there's a place for everyone in our school sports programs.

Scout club

Scouting is a global youth movement founded over a century ago and has left an indelible mark on generations of young individuals. It has evolved into a multifaceted program, encompassing a wide range of activities and opportunities for personal growth. Joining a Scout club is a decision that can profoundly impact the lives of young people, offering a myriad of benefits that extend well beyond the Scout's years.Helps the student to start focusing on character development, leadership skills, outdoor experiences and a sense of community.

Drama club

Our young, vibrant baby class students are proudly stepping up to represent us at the sub-county levels this year, showcasing their talent, confidence, and the incredible potential of our future generation. With their bright smiles and unstoppable energy, they embody the spirit of growth, learning, and excellence, making us all proud as they take on this exciting challenge.

Indoor Games

We offer various indoor games including darts, badminton and table tennis.This helps our student to keep fit and take a break from books.

Athlete sports
