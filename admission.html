Admission/

Step 1

INTERVIEW-

Your child will be interviewed / tested by the school principal or the relevant assistants to assess the academic level of your child. The outcome of the child’s test will give us a better idea as to whether the child will be appropriately placed.

Step 2

ENROLLMENT-

Once you agree with your child’s placement, payment of the tuition fees guaranties the enrollment of your child in his / her class level. At this point, your child will be formally accepted as a student at Lions academy.

Step 3

Payment

The guardian is then required to full pay the fees and the student is now fully admitted

Step 4

Congrats your child will become a 'Lion'
